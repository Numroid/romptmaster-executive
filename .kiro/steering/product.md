# Product Overview

## PromptMaster Executive

A gamified web application that teaches prompt engineering to senior finance and business leaders through realistic business scenarios. The platform transforms AI interaction skills training into an engaging, business-focused learning experience.

## Core Value Proposition

- **Problem:** Finance executives struggle with AI tools due to poor prompt engineering skills
- **Solution:** Gamified learning through familiar business contexts (budget reviews, M&A analysis, board presentations)
- **Impact:** 300% increase in executive AI productivity, 50% reduction in time-to-insight

## Target Users

- Senior finance professionals (CFOs, VPs, Directors, Managers)
- Business leaders seeking AI productivity improvements
- Enterprise teams requiring AI skills development

## Key Features

- Interactive business scenario simulations
- Real-time AI response feedback and scoring
- Progressive skill development with gamification
- Business-themed achievements and progress tracking
- Template library for practical application
- Team collaboration and leaderboards

## Success Metrics

- 80% monthly active user retention
- 90% users achieve "Practitioner" level within 30 days
- 40% reported time savings on AI-assisted tasks
- $500K ARR target within 12 months
